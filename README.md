# Growth systems case studies

Write-ups of systems I have designed and built across revenue operations, CRM,
marketing automation, and independent products.

The internal systems are sanitised: no credentials, customer records, workflow
exports, or confidential business logic. The product case studies focus on the
problem, operating model, and design decisions rather than publishing private data.

## Revenue and lifecycle systems

1. [The lead-gen agent: scrape, dedupe, outreach, CRM](01-lead-gen-agent.md) —
   an always-on B2B acquisition pipeline that business development teams run through Slack.

2. [Campaign auto-setup: from hours to minutes](02-campaign-auto-setup.md) —
   one brief generates and routes multi-channel campaigns, with AI drafting and human approval.
   This study documents the historical seven-market setup; my current group scope is six APAC markets.

## Independent product builds

3. [Bloom: hotel sales workspace](03-bloom-sales-workspace.md) —

4. [Crape POS: Thai-first shop operations](04-crape-pos.md) —
   a mobile point-of-sale and profit tracker designed around how a small crepe shop works.

5. [RentBill: recurring apartment billing](05-rentbill.md) —
   a work-in-progress billing product where physical meter history and tenant debt follow different rules.

## What I optimise for

- Start with recurring operational friction, not a preferred tool
- Keep people in the interface they already use
- Put human review between AI output and consequential actions
- Make ownership, failure states, and handover part of the system
- Measure adoption, time returned, and business outcomes
